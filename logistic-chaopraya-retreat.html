Logistic Chaopraya Retreat

Project Year: 2025
Skills & Applications:
Rhino · Grasshopper · AutoCAD · V-Ray · Adobe Photoshop · Adobe Illustrator · Adobe InDesign
Environmental Analysis · Physical Model Making

Logistic Chaopraya Retreat reimagines a riverside logistics hub along the Chao Phraya River as a tranquil, adaptive retreat. Anchored by refurbished shipping containers and suspended walkways, the design weaves public green spaces through loading bays, offering commuters and dockworkers shaded rest areas overlooking the water. Passive‐cooling pergolas and modular bamboo screens respond to shifting seasonal winds, while floating platforms double as performance stages and community gardens. Careful reuse of industrial materials and site‐specific plantings create a hybrid landscape that balances operational efficiency with human comfort—transforming a functional port into an engaging, resilient public realm.
