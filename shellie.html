Shellie

Project Year: 2025
Skills & Applications:
Fashion Design · Textile Pattern Making · Branding & Identity ·
Adobe Illustrator · Adobe Photoshop · Physical Prototyping

Shellie is where soft nostalgia and contemporary whimsy intertwine—an artisanal fashion label dedicated to delicate details, handcrafted touches, and timeless charm. Styled with a pleated lace skirt, silver watch, and cloud-blue bag, Shellie’s debut “Gleam Draped Top” is your soft summer statement—perfect for gallery strolls, afternoon cafés, and golden-hour moments. Soft femininity meets playful nostalgia in every piece: a fashion brand inspired by delicate charm and vintage details, reinvented for today’s confident, dreamy girls. Shellie balances innocence and individuality with timeless silhouettes and a whimsical polka-dot motif, offering handcrafted, small-batch garments that celebrate personal style and story. Rooted in sustainable practices and artisan craftsmanship, each design is made to be mixed, matched, and layered—inviting wearers to create their own moments of magic and meaning.
